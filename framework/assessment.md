### Scoring

**2 Yes:** You're ahead but have gaps — strengthen visibility
**1 Yes:** You're at risk — prioritize tracking
**0 Yes:** You're flying blind — start immediately

## Recommended Actions by Score

### Score 0-14: Immediate Actions

1. **This week:** List all AI initiatives in a spreadsheet
2. **Next week:** Identify owners for each
3. **Month 1:** Implement basic cost tracking
4. **Month 2:** Add security controls
5. **Month 3:** Establish monthly review

### Score 15-24: Foundation Building

1. **Month 1:** Complete initiative inventory
2. **Month 2:** Implement Layer 1 monitoring
3. **Month 3:** Add governance basics
4. **Month 4:** Begin Layer 2 standardization

### Score 25-34: Gap Closing

1. **Identify:** Which section scored lowest?
2. **Prioritize:** Top 3 gaps in that section
3. **Implement:** One improvement per month
4. **Measure:** Track score improvement quarterly

### Score 35-44: Optimization

1. **Analyze:** Where are the remaining gaps?
2. **Benchmark:** Compare to industry standards
3. **Optimize:** Focus on efficiency gains
4. **Scale:** Expand successful patterns

### Score 45-54: Excellence

1. **Maintain:** Keep processes running
2. **Share:** Document and share learnings
3. **Innovate:** Explore advanced capabilities
4. **Lead:** Help others in your organization
