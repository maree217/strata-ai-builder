## Initiative Stages

### Stage 1: Idea

```
Status: Captured and prioritized
Owner: Business sponsor identified
Action: Evaluate feasibility
```

**Key Questions:**
- What problem does this solve?
- What's the expected value?
- What's the risk level?

### Stage 2: Experiment (Alpha)

```
Status: Proof of concept in progress
Owner: Technical lead + business sponsor
Action: Validate assumptions
```

**Key Questions:**
- Is the technology feasible?
- What's the actual cost?
- Does it solve the problem?

### Stage 3: Pilot (Beta)

```
Status: Limited production deployment
Owner: Product owner + operations
Action: Validate at scale
```

**Key Questions:**
- Does it work with real users?
- What's the actual ROI?
- What needs improvement?

### Stage 4: Production (Live)

```
Status: Full deployment
Owner: Operations team
Action: Monitor and iterate
```

**Key Questions:**
- Is it meeting targets?
- What's the trend?
- What's next?

### Stage 5: Sunset

```
Status: Scheduled for retirement
Owner: Operations + governance
Action: Graceful shutdown
```

**Key Questions:**
- Is there a replacement?
- Are dependencies handled?
- Is data archived properly?

## Governance Integration

Portfolio Intelligence isn't just tracking. It's active governance.

### Monthly Review Agenda

1. **Portfolio Health** (10 min)
   - Total initiatives by stage
   - Movement since last review
   - Risk distribution

2. **Performance Deep-Dive** (20 min)
   - Top 3 performers: Why?
   - Bottom 3: What's wrong?
   - At-risk initiatives: Action needed?

3. **Resource Allocation** (15 min)
   - Budget vs. actual
   - Team capacity
   - Investment priorities

4. **Compliance Check** (10 min)
   - Documentation status
   - Audit findings
   - Upcoming requirements

5. **Decisions** (5 min)
   - New initiatives approved
   - Initiatives to sunset
   - Resource reallocations

## Common Anti-Patterns

### Anti-Pattern 1: "Set and Forget"

**Symptom:** AI deployed and never reviewed
**Result:** Degrading performance, cost overruns, compliance drift
**Fix:** Monthly portfolio reviews

### Anti-Pattern 2: "Shadow AI"

**Symptom:** Initiatives running without visibility
**Result:** Unknown risk exposure, duplicate efforts, wasted spend
**Fix:** Central portfolio tracking

### Anti-Pattern 3: "Zombie Initiatives"

**Symptom:** Projects neither active nor closed
**Result:** Resource drain, confusion, technical debt
**Fix:** Clear stage gates and sunset process

### Anti-Pattern 4: "Hero Dependency"

**Symptom:** One person knows everything about an initiative
**Result:** Key-person risk, knowledge silos
**Fix:** Documented ownership and knowledge transfer
